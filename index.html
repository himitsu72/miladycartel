Welcome to Milady Cartel!

Milady Cartel is a decentralized NFT market-making protocol and metegovernance subDAO utilizing bond and rebase mechanisms with a treasury strategy focused on the acquisition of floor Miladys, which are then deployed in various yield strategies, which will initially be the MILADY NFTX vault. Milady Maker NFT is superior tech and an elevated financial instrument and we believe the first protocol to acquire 1000 floor Miladys will cement themselves as a formidable DAO with a large treasury, as well as fill the niche of NFT DAO’s targeting high yield investment strategies over the traditional “Blue Chip” investment thesis. With a milady focused strategy, yields returned to protocol participants will be approximately 3000% higher than those currently offered by alternatives such as FloorDAO.

Why Milady?

Miladys are the first NFT to experience true runaway velocity. NFTs with network effects orders of magnitude in size are only generating a fraction of the yield select Milady holders are currently appreciating. This is for several reasons: superior art, highly tradeable floors, hardline CC0, and strong community. Almost 10% of the supply is staked in vaults earning high yield, and on NFTX, Milady has the highest staked/floor/supply ratio out of any collection.
